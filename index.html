About Arena.Social

Arena.Social is a decentralized engagement marketplace built on the Avalanche ecosystem. We connect content creators who need genuine social interaction with active community members who are willing to perform high-quality engagement for rewards.

Dual Token Economy: $ARENA and $eARENA

  • $ARENA (Real Token): This is the governance and primary utility token on the Avalanche chain. Its live price is fetched dynamically.
  • $eARENA (Ecosystem Token): This is the internal, secure token used for all transactions within the platform (funding tasks, rewarding users). Users **must first send $ARENA** to the official Treasury Wallet: 0xED0e6b41B09168fFB9c5f562C0AB47FB37D84310. We verify the transaction hash (TxID) to issue $eARENA, ensuring token safety. Users can claim their $eARENA back to $ARENA weekly.

Quality Control & Verification

To ensure creators receive the best results, every task requires **manual approval** by the creator. When a user submits a proof (like a comment link), the task creator must verify the quality and legitimacy of the action in the **Approvals** dashboard before the payout is triggered. This puts the creator in charge of ensuring high-quality engagement. If the creator does not approve within a set time period (2 days in our system), the proof is automatically approved, ensuring timely payment to the engager.
