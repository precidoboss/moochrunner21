What is ClipDAO?

ClipDAO is a decentralized bounty platform where streamers lock AVAX into smart contract escrow and clippers compete to make the best clips. No informal agreements. No waiting to get paid. Built specifically for Blaze and Arena streamers.

🤝 Hire Me — Direct Clipper Deals

Hire Me lets streamers directly hire individual clippers outside the open bounty system. All terms are negotiated on-chain and payment is held in escrow until clips are approved.

📡 For Streamers
1. Browse Clippers page
2. View clipper's portfolio (POW)
3. Send an offer with clip count + AVAX
4. Negotiate until both parties agree
5. Lock AVAX — deal activates
6. Review & approve each submitted clip
7. All approved → clipper is auto-paid
✂️ For Clippers
1. Set your Hire Me profile to Available
2. Upload POW clips to your portfolio
3. Set your rate note & bio
4. Wait for streamer offers
5. Counter-offer or accept terms
6. Submit clips one at a time
7. Get paid when all approved
Cancellation: Either party can request cancellation at any time. The other party must agree for it to take effect. If agreed, the streamer gets their AVAX back minus a 2% penalty. If rejected, the deal continues.
💰

Genesis Holder Revenue Share

Genesis NFT holders receive 30% of all treasury fees every month, weighted by NFT level. The more active you are, the higher your level, the bigger your share.

FAQ

Do I need crypto to use ClipDAO?
You need AVAX on Avalanche Fuji. New users get 1 free trial action. To continue, mint an NFT for 0.1 AVAX (Early Access).
Which wallet?
Core Wallet (built for Avalanche) or MetaMask. ClipDAO auto-adds Fuji testnet on connect.
How does Hire Me differ from bounties?
Bounties are open competitions — any clipper can submit. Hire Me is a private deal between one streamer and one clipper with negotiated terms and dedicated escrow.
Zero submissions bounty?
If zero submissions exist at deadline, you can cancel and your AVAX is fully refunded.
Where to post clips?
Post to X (Twitter) or YouTube, then submit that public link. Clip must be from Blaze or Arena content.
