How ClipDAO Works

A trustless marketplace connecting streamers on Blaze and Arena with skilled clippers — powered by Avalanche smart contracts. No middlemen. Automatic AVAX payouts. On-chain reputation that compounds.

What is ClipDAO?

ClipDAO is a decentralized bounty platform where streamers lock AVAX into smart contract escrow and clippers compete to make the best clips. No informal agreements. No waiting to get paid. No ghosting. The smart contract holds the funds and releases them automatically when a winner is selected.

It's built specifically around Blaze and Arena — the two streaming platforms where the ClipDAO community is active.

The Full Flow

1
Mint Your NFT
Every participant — clipper or streamer — needs a ClipDAO membership NFT. Genesis NFT (500 max, 0.5 AVAX) or Early Access NFT (0.1 AVAX). Your NFT is your identity, your voting power, and your reputation score all in one.
2
Streamer Posts a Bounty
A streamer goes live on Blaze or Arena, then comes to ClipDAO to post a bounty. They specify: their stream link, which platform it's on, what kind of clips they want, any special requirements, the AVAX reward, the deadline (24/48/72hrs), how many winners, the max submissions per clipper (1–3), and whether they pick the winner or the community votes.
🔒 The AVAX is locked into the smart contract escrow the moment the bounty goes live. The streamer cannot cancel once submissions exist.
3
Clippers Watch & Clip
Clippers watch the streamer's Blaze or Arena stream (live or VOD), find the best moments, and create clips. They post those clips to X (Twitter) or YouTube. The clip must be a real public post — the link is what gets submitted.
4
Submit the Clip Link
The clipper comes to ClipDAO and submits their X or YouTube link on the bounty page. The link is timestamped on-chain. Each clipper can submit up to the max number of clips the streamer set (1, 2, or 3). You can't submit the same link twice and you can't submit to your own bounty.
5
Winner Selected
Streamer Picks: The streamer reviews all submissions and clicks to select the winner(s). Payout fires instantly.

Community Vote: NFT holders vote during the open window. Genesis holders have 3x voting power, Early Access 1.5x. When the deadline passes, anyone can trigger resolution and the top voted clips win.
6
Automatic Payout
The smart contract sends AVAX directly to winner wallet(s). Platform takes 4% automatically. No manual transfer. No waiting. The clipper's reputation score and NFT level both increase on-chain.

FAQ

Do I need crypto to use ClipDAO?
Yes. You need AVAX on Avalanche to mint an NFT and to post bounties. Clippers only need an NFT (0.1 AVAX) to start submitting — no gas needed to vote or submit on Fuji testnet where gas is free test tokens.
Which wallet should I use?
Core Wallet (built for Avalanche) or MetaMask. ClipDAO auto-adds the Fuji testnet when you connect.
Can a clipper also be a streamer?
Yes. Your NFT role is your primary role but you can do both. You just can't submit clips to your own bounties.
What if no one submits to my bounty?
If zero submissions exist when the deadline passes, you can cancel the bounty and your AVAX is fully refunded.
Where do I post my clips?
Post to X (Twitter) or YouTube. Then submit that public link here. The clip must be from the streamer's Blaze or Arena content.
